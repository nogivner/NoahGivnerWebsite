Homeworkquest

Your Fantasy Adventure - Transform your homework into quests. Gamify your academic tasks with calendar tracking, character progression, and class-based quest givers.

VIEW PROJECT

OVERVIEW

Game-Based Productivity

Homeworkquest turns your academic responsibilities into an engaging fantasy adventure. Complete quests, level up your character, and maintain your streak while managing your coursework. All tasks are automatically synced and backed up with Google Calendar integration.

Calendar View

Visual calendar interface displaying all your tasks and quests organized by due date with color-coded categories.

Quest Givers

Classes are represented as Quest Givers, each with unique icons and completion tracking for all assignments.

Character Progression

Track your level, experience points, completed quests, and maintain daily streaks to build long-term habits.

Task Management

Create, edit, and organize tasks with custom categories, priorities, and completion status tracking.

Gamification

Earn achievements, badges, and rewards as you complete quests and maintain your academic journey.

Color-Coded System

Each task features a color-coded visual system with icons to help you quickly identify and prioritize your work.

Google Calendar Sync

Automatic integration with Google Calendar for seamless backup and synchronization of all your tasks and events.

DESIGN

Key Features

Homeworkquest combines productivity with entertainment to make managing your academic workload more engaging.

Calendar Management

Visual Calendar - Intuitive calendar grid showing all your tasks

Today Highlight - Clear indication of the current day

Task Badges - Visual indicators showing task counts and priorities

Google Calendar Sync - Automatic backup to Google Calendar

Quest System

Class-Based Quest Givers - Each class is a unique quest giver

Color-Coded Tasks - Different colors for different task types

Progress Tracking - Track completed vs total quests per class

Priority Indicators - Visual markers for urgent or special tasks

User Experience Highlights

Homeworkquest features a dark, elegant UI with gold accents that creates an immersive fantasy gaming experience while maintaining productivity focus.

Character Stats Panel

Track your progress with detailed character statistics including level, experience points, completed quests, and current streak.

Navigation & Sidebar

Easy access to Calendar, Quest Log, Character profile, Store, and Settings from the left sidebar with intuitive icons.

Quest Givers Footer

Quick view of all your classes showing completion progress (X/Y completed) with unique icons for each subject.

Quick Actions

Bottom bar with add button for quick task creation, plus logout and app close options for session management.

Data Protection & Sync

All events and quests are automatically backed up to Google Calendar, ensuring your data is safe and accessible across devices.
